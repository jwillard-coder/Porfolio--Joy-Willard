About

Hello, my name is Joy Willard. I’m a 20 year old graphic designer and illustrator who loves creating artwork with deep meaning. I enjoy exploring different forms of typography, and I see type as seasons each piece deserves its own unique style rather than repeating the same fonts for every design. When I face creative blocks, I draw inspiration from my dreams or visual references I find on Pinterest. I’m a very dedicated artist, I always push myself to do my best, because I don’t feel fulfilled unless I truly love the final outcome. To align my work with a client’s brand, I study the recurring elements in their designs and apply those details to their logo, website, or project to keep everything consistent. My favorite projects are character designs I love creating characters, bringing them to life, and exploring color to express their personality.
